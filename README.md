# 💧 AquaGuard AI
Smart Water Quality Monitoring & Early Warning System (SDG 6 – Clean Water & Sanitation).

### 🔗 Live Link
[View the Live Web App](https://your-app-name.streamlit.app)

### 🌟 Key Features
* **Explainable AI (XAI)**: Understand exactly why water is classified as Safe, Medium, or High Risk with clear parameter rule breakdowns and contribution charts.
* **Historical Data Module**: Track and filter previous analyses with sortable tables and 5 Plotly interactive charts.
* **Persona-based Warnings**: Custom safety actions tailored for Homes, Schools, and Hospitals.
